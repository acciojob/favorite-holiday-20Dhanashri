Kerala


Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is famous for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, rich culture, wildlife, and breathtaking natural beauty.

  1. Munnar – famous for its beautiful tea plantations.
  2. Alleppey Backwaters – a peaceful houseboat cruising experience.
  3. Kovalam Beach – a popular seaside destination.
  4. Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary – home to diverse flora and fauna.
  5. Wayanad – known for waterfalls, caves, and scenic views.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry – a traditional Kerala breakfast dish.
  • Appam with Stew – soft rice pancakes served with vegetable or meat stew.
  • Kerala Sadya – a full meal served on a banana leaf.
  • Karimeen Fry – a delicious fish preparation.
  • Prawn Curry – flavorful seafood cooked with aromatic spices.
